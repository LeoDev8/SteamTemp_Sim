# GRE Quantitative Vocabulary (Score Compensation Tool)

## 1. Number Theory (Numbers & Operations)
| English Term | Chinese | Note |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Integer / Distinct Integer** | 整数 / 不同的整数 | 0 is an integer, but not positive/negative. |
| **Prime Number** | 质数 | 1 is NOT prime. 2 is the smallest prime. |
| **Factor / Multiple** | 因数 / 倍数 | 12 is a multiple of 3. |
| **Remainder / Quotient** | 余数 / 商 | 7 divided by 3 has a remainder of 1. |
| **Reciprocal** | 倒数 | Reciprocal of 4 is 1/4. |
