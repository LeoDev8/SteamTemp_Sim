# Detailed Execution Guide: From Power Plant to Germany 2027

## 1. Language & Academic Certification (2026.05 - 2026.12)

### 1.1 IELTS (The Entry Ticket)
*   **Official Portal**: [NEEA IELTS Registration](https://ielts.neea.edu.cn/)
*   **Selection**: Choose **Academic (A)**. *Note: "IELTS for UKVI" is also accepted and has more flexible backup options.*
*   **Location**: Any provincial capital city (Nanjing, Hefei, etc.).
*   **Cost**: ~2,170 RMB.
*   **Strategy**: Take the exam by **August 2026**.

### 1.2 APS Audit (The Gatekeeper)
*   **Official Portal**: [APS China (aps.org.cn)](https://www.aps.org.cn/)
*   **Procedure**: 
    1. Register online & pay **2,500 RMB**.
    2. Mail notarized documents (Degree, Transcript) to Beijing.
    3. Wait for the interview (usually 2-4 months).
*   **Interview Location**: Beijing or Shanghai.
*   **Interview Prep**: Focus on "Circuit Analysis" and "Power System Analysis". Use `SteamTemp_Sim` as a portfolio to prove technical competence.

### 1.3 GRE (The GPA Compensator)
*   **Official Portal**: [ETS GRE Registration](https://gre.neea.edu.cn/)
*   **Timeline**: Prepare during the APS waiting period. Take the exam in **Nov/Dec 2026**.
*   **Target**: **Quantitative 168-170** is mandatory to offset the Linear Algebra makeup grade.

## 2. University Applications (2027.01 - 2027.05)

### 2.1 Application Channels
*   **Uni-assist (UA)**: A third-party portal used by many universities (e.g., TU Dresden).
*   **Direct Portals**: Top universities like **RWTH Aachen** use their own systems (e.g., **RWTHonline**).
*   **Action**: Create accounts on these portals as soon as the application window opens (usually March for Winter Semester).

### 2.2 Motivation Letter (Statement of Purpose)
*   **Core Strategy**: Focus on the "Engineer Persona".
    - **Drafting**: Describe how 3 years of field experience led you to pursue automation.
    - **Highlight**: Explicitly mention and link your GitHub repository (`LeoDev8/SteamTemp_Sim`).
    - **Goal**: Show that you have solved real-world problems with code.

## 3. Visa & Financial Security (2027.06 - 2027.08)

### 3.1 Financial Preparation (Blocked Account)
*   **Providers**: [Expatrio](https://www.expatrio.com/) or [Fintiba](https://www.fintiba.com/).
*   **Amount**: ~11,904 EUR (Required by law for 2027).
*   **Process**: Transfer the funds to the German account -> Receive "Opening Confirmation" for Visa.

### 3.2 National Visa (Type D)
*   **Type**: You are applying for a **Long-term National Visa**, NOT a Schengen (Tourist) visa.
*   **Documents**: Admission Letter, Blocked Account confirmation, Insurance, Passport.
*   **Flight/Hotel Logic**: 
    - **Flights**: NOT required before visa approval. Use a "flight reservation" if needed. Only buy the real ticket after the visa is in hand.
    - **Accommodation**: Provide a temporary address (Youth Hostel or temporary sublet). No need for long-term lease proof.

## 4. Final Transition (2027.09 - 2027.10)

### 4.1 Resignation Logic
*   **Golden Rule**: **DO NOT resign until your Visa is stamped in your passport.**
*   **Handover**: Complete your final shifts at the power plant professionally.

### 4.2 Flight & Landing
*   **Date**: Aim to arrive in Germany by late September.
*   **Insurance**: Use the "Incoming Insurance" from Expatrio for the first 90 days, then switch to public insurance (TK/AOK) upon university enrollment.
